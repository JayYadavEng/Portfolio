Columbia Space Initiative

Background

The Rocketry team within the Columbia Space Initiative focuses on design/build competitions against other schools. In the past two years, we have participated in the Spaceport America Cup and FAR-OUT missions. I have been on the airframe subteam, which focuses on the exterior structure of the rocket and overall weight and balance.

Design Process

On the airframe team, we start with a general idea of the rocket requirements, and use tools like OpenRocket to finalize the layout. We find the appropriate nose and fin structure through FEA in Ansys Mechanical and CFD in Ansys Fluent. We then construct the components from carbon fiber, performing layups and CNC waterjet machining to get the finished product.

Competition

Over the past two years, we've had our first two successful launches. While in 2023 our landing was less than controlled, we made significant improvements and had our first successful recovery last year the FAR-OUT competition.
